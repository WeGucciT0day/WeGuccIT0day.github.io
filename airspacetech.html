About Me

Hiya! I am AirspaceTech. Welcome to my Information Center as well as a portfolio! Here, you can find everything about me.

My interests mainly involve types of transportation, in particular, automobiles and aviation.

I was first employed in the Ro-Aviation industry on August 17th, 2021. I was employed with FlyHaven Airways, still one of my greatest accomplishments to date. I then was promoted to Flight Host via an application about 3 weeks later, and about 3 months after that I became a Director. Then, In December, I took on the role of Flight Operations/Hosting Head, as I had around 50-60 flights of experience hosting.

Around September, I was interested in starting my own airline. I named it "WorldJet", which allowed for freedom of where the departure airports were.

This airline is still in development and should conclude development in around June 2022. I am very excited to begin my journey as the owner of an airline!

I then later became interested in UI, and decided to begin making ROBLOX Studio UI. I was then assisted in my journey by many large Ro-Aviation corporations. I did the UI for the FlyIslamabad Ordering System, as well as the FlyHaven HavenMiles System.

That is all, thank you for reading!

Commission Pricing

My pricing is determined based on the number of frames and the complexity. Your complexity will be determined by AirspaceTech. For now, I only accept payment in robux. My pricing sheet is below:

Low Complexity (300 robux per frame):

  • 1-3 Frames - 300 -900 Robux

  • 4-6 Frames - 1200 - 1800 Robux

  • 7-10 Frames - 2,100 - 3,000 Robux


Medium Complexity (600 robux per frame):

  • 1-3 Frames - 600 - 1,800 Robux

  • 4-6 Frames - 2,400 - 3,600 Robux

  • 7-10 Frames - 4,200 - 6,000 Robux


High Complexity (1000 robux per frame):

  • 1-3 Frames - 1,000 - 3,000 Robux

  • 4-6 Frames - 4,000 - 6,000 Robux

  • 7-10 Frames - 7,000 - 10,000 Robux


ScreenGUIs will automatically be High Complexity+.


All other complexity levels (if needed) will be quoted manually by AirspaceTech.

Commission Policies

Types of Order Policies

  • All orders are made in ROBLOX Studio, nowhere else

  • All orders must follow ROBLOX TOS

  • A detailed description of each of the frames must be provided in order to avoid miscommunication, delays, and the order not turning out the way you wanted it to

  • UIs which have already been started and need to be completed will still fall under normal pricing, no discounts will be applied

Payment Policies

  • I agree to pay 50 percent of the price of the UI before it is started, this is to avoid people wasting AirspaceTech's time

  • I agree that if I am unable to pay the second 50 percent within 7 days of the UI's completion, AirspaceTech has full rights to auction off my UI design and not provide a refund for the original 50 percent

  • I agree that the quote that is given to me does not include marketplace fees if being paid via t-shirt or game pass, therefore if I plan on paying by this method I (the buyer) will compensate for these fees

  • I agree that I will not be refunded my original 50 percent if I cancel more than halfway through the order's completion, and the half-finished UI will also be auctioned off

  • I agree that no discounts will be offered due to the UI being completed late

  • I agree that if AirspaceTech is unable to complete my UI that he will refund all of the Robux back. However, this refund may be delayed as AirspaceTech WILL compensate you for the marketplace fees


Ordering Policies

  • I understand that the timeline given for the completion of the UI can be changed at any time

  • I understand that I will not rush AirspaceTech as he is busy, takes other orders which may have priority, and is employed in a variety of industries

  • I understand that AirspaceTech can cancel my order for any reason he deems fit, with or without providing any compensation
